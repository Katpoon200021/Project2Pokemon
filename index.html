*Pokemon Black and White*

The Unova region is a region of the Pokemon world and the setting for the Pokemon DS games: Pokemon Black and White and Pokemon Black 2 and White 2. The Pokemon that inhabit Unova are diverse and different from those of the other regions, like Kanto (Pokemon Red and Blue), Johto (Pokemon Gold and Silver), Hoenn (Pokemon Ruby and Sapphire), and Sinnoh (Pokemon Diamond, Pearl, and Platinum).
